📖 How Grades Work

📊 Weighted Categories

Your overall grade is calculated using weighted categories: Formative Activities (10%) for in-lesson practice, Assignments (30%) for unit tasks and projects, Performance Tasks (35%) for major demonstrations of learning, and Quizzes (25%) for knowledge checks.

📈 Current Grade

Your grade is based on completed and graded items only. As you finish more work, your grade will adjust. Missing items are tracked separately and may affect your grade if not submitted.

🔄 Reassessment

For quizzes, your highest score counts if you've made multiple attempts. For assignments marked "Needs Revision," you can resubmit for a better mark. Performance tasks may allow revision at the teacher's discretion.

🤖 AI Feedback

Some written responses are scored by AI first to give you instant feedback. These marks are labelled "AI Feedback" and may be reviewed and adjusted by your teacher. Final marks are labelled "Teacher Reviewed" or "Finalized."

⏳ Pending Review

Items marked "Submitted — Pending Review" have been received but haven't been graded yet. You'll see your mark once your teacher (or AI) reviews your work.

❓ Missing Work

Missing assignments are highlighted so you can catch up. They aren't counted in your current average, but your teacher will follow up on unsubmitted work. Get it in as soon as you can!
